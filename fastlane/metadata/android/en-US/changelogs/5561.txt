MCP/agent settings cleanup:

• The two per-connection MCP toggles are now grouped together and clearly labelled by direction — "Allow agent to control this connection" (inbound: can the agent drive this session) and "Reverse-expose Haven's MCP to this host" (outbound: reverse-tunnel Haven's MCP back to the remote host).
