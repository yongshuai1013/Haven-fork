v5.43.2 — desktop-over-SSH lifecycle fixes

• Fix a family of bugs where a VNC/RDP desktop tunneled over SSH could
  leave an orphaned session or tab behind. Disconnecting the connection
  from the Connections list now also closes its Desktop tab; a failed
  connect (e.g. no server listening) no longer leaves the SSH session
  lit with a green dot; and a dropped tunnel tears the tab down with it.

• The Connections list now reflects the dependency: a VNC/RDP-over-SSH
  profile nests under its SSH tunnel and shows its own connected
  indicator while its desktop is open.

• Under the hood, a desktop tab and the SSH session that carries it are
  now tied together by a single lease, so teardown cascades both ways
  instead of each disconnect path having to clean up by hand.
