🗂️ Reopening a workspace is reliable now. Restoring several terminals no longer stalls on the session picker or stacks duplicate tabs: each host comes up once, its tmux/zellij sessions attach over that one connection, and hosts start in parallel so a slow or prompting one can't block the rest. Relaunching reuses what's live; a session closed on the host is reattached, not empty; older workspaces pin their names on first restore. Save current now labels rows "host tmux name".
