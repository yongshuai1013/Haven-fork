Security fix: turning off biometric protection on a private key — or the global app lock — now requires authentication itself, so the protection can't be stripped and the key exported without a prompt. You can also import your existing rclone remotes straight from a Linux rclone.conf: paste it or pick the file, then choose which remotes to add. And YubiKey / security-key SSH auth is far more robust on flaky links — a stalled connection no longer snowballs into a backlog of failed FIDO attempts, and a key exported over USB/IP no longer contends with on-device auth for the same key.
